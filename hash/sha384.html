What is SHA-384?

SHA-384 is a cryptographic hash function that produces a 384-bit (48-byte) hash value. It is part of the SHA-2 family of hashing algorithms, which are widely used for data integrity and security purposes. The SHA-384 algorithm is known for its strong resistance to collision attacks, making it an excellent choice for applications that require high levels of security.
