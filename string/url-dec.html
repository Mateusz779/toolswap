What is URL Decoding?

URL encoding is a process that converts special characters in URLs into a format that can be transmitted over the internet. This ensures that URLs remain intact without any misinterpretation by web browsers. However, sometimes you may need to decode these URLs to understand their original content. Our URL Decoder Tool simplifies this process, making it easy to convert encoded URLs back to their original form.
