In the world of programming and data formatting, text transformation plays a crucial role in ensuring consistency and readability. One of the popular text formats is Upper Camel Case, also known as Pascal Case. What is Upper Camel Case?

Upper Camel Case is a convention where each word in a phrase starts with a capital letter, and there are no spaces or punctuation marks. For example, the phrase "convert text to upper camel case" becomes "ConvertTextToUpperCamelCase." This format enhances readability and provides a clear structure, especially in programming contexts.
