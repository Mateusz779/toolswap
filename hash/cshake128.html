What is cSHAKE128?

cSHAKE128 is a variant of the SHAKE family of extendable-output functions (XOFs). It utilizes a cryptographic sponge construction to produce variable-length outputs, making it versatile for various applications, including digital signatures, password hashing, and data integrity verification. Its security and flexibility make it a popular choice in modern cryptographic protocols.
