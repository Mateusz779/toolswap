Understanding Power Units: kW and HP

Kilowatts (kW) and horsepower (HP) are common units of power used globally. Kilowatts are part of the metric system, while horsepower is a unit widely used in the United States and the UK. Understanding how to convert between these units is essential for anyone working with engines, motors, or any machinery involving power output.

How to Convert kW to HP and Vice Versa

  • Converting HP to kW: If you need to go the other way, enter the horsepower value, and our converter will give you the corresponding kW value. This is helpful when dealing with specifications that list power in horsepower but you need the metric equivalent.

Practical Applications
