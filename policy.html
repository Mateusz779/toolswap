Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 4, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to ToolSwap, accessible at https://toolswap.xyz. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application, which includes various converters, hashing functions, and other tools. By using ToolSwap, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Client-Side Operations

ToolSwap operates entirely on the client side. This means that all operations, including file conversions and hashing functions, are performed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, files, or other input data to our servers. Your privacy is maintained, and your data remains on your device.

Analytics

To improve our services, we use Google Analytics and Plausible Analytics. These tools help us understand how users interact with our application. The data collected through these services is anonymized and does not include any personally identifiable information.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit our application, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to ToolSwap. Google Analytics uses cookies to track user interactions. You can learn more about Google Analytics and its privacy practices here.

Plausible Analytics

Plausible Analytics is a privacy-friendly alternative to Google Analytics. It does not use cookies and is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations. For more information on how Plausible handles data, please visit Plausible's privacy policy.

Cookies

ToolSwap uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze usage patterns. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the application.

Data Security

We take data security seriously. Since ToolSwap does not store any personal data or files on our servers, the risk of data breaches is significantly reduced. However, we encourage users to take appropriate measures to secure their devices and data.

Third-Party Links

Our application may contain links to third-party websites. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those sites, and we are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website. Your continued use of ToolSwap after any changes indicates your acceptance of the new terms.

Contact Us

Email: contact@toolswap.xyz

Thank you for using ToolSwap! Your privacy is our priority, and we are committed to protecting your information.
