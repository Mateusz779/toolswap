What is BLAKE2s?

BLAKE2s is a cryptographic hash function that is faster than MD5, SHA-1, and SHA-2, while providing a high level of security. It is particularly suitable for applications where speed is crucial, such as in hashing passwords or generating unique identifiers. BLAKE2s is optimized for 8 to 32-bit platforms, making it an excellent choice for various applications.
