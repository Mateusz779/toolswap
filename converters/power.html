Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • What is the difference between horsepower and kilowatts? Horsepower and kilowatts are both units of power, but they measure different things. One horsepower is equivalent to approximately 0.746 kilowatts. Understanding this conversion is vital for comparing engine power and electrical output.

  • How do I convert BTU/h to kW? To convert BTU/h to kilowatts, divide the BTU/h value by 3,412. This conversion is essential in HVAC applications where power output is often measured in BTUs.

  • What is the most common power unit used in my country? The most common unit of power varies by region. For example, watts are widely used in most countries, while horsepower is more prevalent in automotive contexts, particularly in the United States.
